Background To The Gala Night Event

It's not a secret that the year 2015 will be a pivotal year in determining the direction of Nigeria. The nation stands at a defining moment that will shape her politics and the social fabric within which her citizens exist.

Nigeria Dialogue intends to lead a nationwide campaign in 2014 to enlighten the Nigerian public about good governance and frame the key issues facing the nation before the general elections.

We plan to work with USAID, Amnesty International and other NGO’s in monitoring the 2015 elections to ensure a fair election.

 

Why Do We Need A Gala Night?

The gala night is an opportunity for professionals, entrepreneurs and philanthropists to enjoy a night of entertainment, networking, fine dining, games and dancing while working to support a worthy cause.

The event will raise awareness for Nigeria Dialogue projects and to raise funds to support them. Nigeria Dialogue aims to plan fund raising events in 5 major cities across 3 continents namely London, Atlanta, New York, Houston and Lagos to support our 2014 events. We plan to continuously engage Nigerians in these cities through our Gala nights, Balls and Town hall meetings.
