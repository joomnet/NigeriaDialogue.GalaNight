What is Nigeria-Dialogue?

Nigeria-Dialogue is a hub for progressive intellectuals who intend to challenge systemic and institutional realities in Nigeria. ND is a platform for forward thinking Nigerians to proffer inputs into sustainable economic, infrastructural and social development through meaningful dialogues and measurable actions.


Who is behind Nigeria-Dialogue?

We are a group of intelligent Nigerians, with a simple idea of bringing change to Nigeria – one person at a time. We are a mix of analytical and creative thinking individuals. We have a reasonable representation in London, New York, Lagos and Abuja.
Our Goals

Serve as a medium to aid in the effective transformation of Nigeria.		 Our Vision

“To build a community of 40 million people who will usher in a civil and developed Nigeria”		 Our Mission

Having conversations that will shape the future of our nation; bringing change to Nigeria – one person at a time!

Our Projects For 2014

Gala Night

The gala night is an opportunity for professionals, entrepreneurs and philanthropists to enjoy a night of entertainment, networking, fine dining, games and dancing while working to support a worthy cause. The event will raise awareness for Nigeria Dialogue projects and to raise funds to support them.

Nigeria Dialogue aims to plan fund raising events in 5 major cities across 3 continents namely London, Atlanta, New York, Houston and Lagos to support our 2014 events. We plan to continuously engage Nigerians in these cities through our Gala nights, Balls and Town hall meetings.

Leadership For Next Generation

This is our work in the community. We intend to visit 25 Cities with higher institutions in Nigeria and host ‘Mega conferences’ attracting 10,000 participants in each conference. This will give us a potential reach of 250,000 Nigerian undergraduates in two years. We will use this platform to teach soft skill, open up job opportunities in the market place, link innovators with venture capitals and encourage participation of first time voters in the process of nation building.

The New School Project

This is a concerted effort of education enthusiasts to revamp the national Secondary and Tertiary curriculums and position Nigerian students for a global future. This is a joint venture between Nigeria Dialogue, the Ministry of Education and a number of key Stakeholders in the private sector.
